Donuts Every Time 2

a Pinball Tournament

Date/Time: March 5th 6pm-12pm
Location: Pauls Pinball Parlour, Anna St, Austin, TX
Format: 15 rounds of swiss style match play (or 4 hours worth). Then gauntlet style finals/semifinals, top 2 seeded players from match play get buys into finals. Half of the remaining players will play through a gauntlet starting with the 4 lowest seeded players, with 2 players moving on to play the next 2 higher seeded players. (Tuesday Jam style semifinals/finals)
Pins: Data East Simpsons, Gottlieb Volley, Gottlieb Genesis, Williams Jokerz!, Your machine here
Prizes: There will be donuts for all and a special donut trophy for the grand champion.
